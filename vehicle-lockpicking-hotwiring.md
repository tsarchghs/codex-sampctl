# Vehicle Lockpicking & Hotwiring

Feature showcase text adapted from a GTA World Forums post for reference.

## Commands

- `/vbreakin` (`/vbi`)  
  Starts the lock minigame.
- `/hotwire`  
  Starts the hotwire minigame.

## Information

These minigames provide a unique way of breaking into private property with
minimal usage of chat commands.

Both minigames have a help text that appears on screen when `H` is pressed, as
described during the process near the top of the interface.

## Difficulty

The difficulty of the minigames may vary depending on:

- Lock Level
- Alarm Level
- Market Price
- Manufacturer
