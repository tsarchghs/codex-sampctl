# Feature Showcase: Los Santos Police Department (LSPD)

- **Author:** Nervous
- **Posted:** January 9, 2018
- **Section:** Features Showcase

## Overview

The LSPD has access to barriers, an updated and highly integrated MDC system, and normal police weaponry. You can request to join the LSPD via recruitment if their applications are open.

## Commands

- `/pduty` — Supplies Health (100), Armour (100), and sets your name tag to police blue.
- `/equipment` — Supplies Health, Armour, Taser, Pistol, and sets your name tag to police blue.
- `/equipment swat` — Supplies Health, Armour, Taser, Combat Pistol, AR-15, and sets your name tag to police blue.
- `/equipment db` — Supplies Health and Pistol.
- `/take #` — Takes a weapon from the cruiser. `#` is the weapon ID (2 = shotgun, 1 = AR, 3 = Sniper (SWAT only)).
- `/takespike` — Takes a spike strip from the cruiser. Must be at the trunk of the vehicle.
- `/spike` — Places a spike strip vertically in front of you, in the direction you are facing.
- `/removespike` — Picks up the spike strip in front of you.
- `/placespike` — Places the spike back into the cruiser. Must be at the trunk of the vehicle.
- `/uniform` — Opens the customization menu for customizing your uniform.
- `/r(adio) [message]` — Transmits a personalized radio transmission to the rest of the Department (private channel).
- `/dep(artment) [message]` — Transmits personalized radio transmissions to all government agencies (PD, EMS, GOV).
- `/m(egaphone) [message]` — Speaks via the vehicle's megaphone, fitted in appropriate faction vehicles.
- `/arrest [player_name]` — Arrests a suspect in the Los Santos Police Department jail cells.
- `/cuff` — Handcuffs the suspect.
- `/uncuff` — Removes the handcuffs from the suspect.
- `/panic` — Alerts all units of your whereabouts via the panic button on your radio or shoulder mic.
- `/MDC` — Opens the in-car computer to run names. Badge number is the login name; password matches the server/UCP password.
- `/radar [dist] [speed]` — Turns the speed radar on. Prompts for distance and speed settings.
- `/radaroff` — Turns the speed radar off. Required when correcting `/radar` parameters.
- `/setpatrol [message]` — Labels your vehicle.
- `/fine [person] [amount] [reason]` — Issues a fine to a player for the specified amount and reason.
- `/vfine [amount] [reason]` — Issues a fine to the closest vehicle for the specified amount and reason.
- `/checkfines [person]` — Returns active fines on the requested person.
- `/checkvehiclefines [leave empty for closest vehicle or enter an ID or part of name]` — Returns active fines on the closest vehicle or the vehicle the requested person is in.
