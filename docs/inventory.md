## Inventory system

**Author:** Nervous
**Original post date:** May 28, 2017 (edited)

### How to open the inventory?

Use **I** to open the personal assistant at any time and then select **Inventory**.

### Text commands (optional)

#### On Foot

- `/myitems` (`/inv`, `/items`, `/inventory`) - List all your current items.
- `/showitems <player>` - Shows your items to a player.
- `/giveitem <player> <itemid> <amount>` - Give an item to another player. You can get the ID with `/myitems`.
- `/useitem` - Use an item and consume it if it is consumable.
- `/deleteitem <itemid> <amount>` - Delete an item from your inventory.
- `/dropitem <itemid> <amount>` - Drop an item on the ground; other players can pick it up.
- **Press Y** - Pick up an item from the ground.

#### In Vehicle

- `/vehmenu` (`/vmenu`) - Menu to manage your vehicle doors, trunk, and inventory.
- `/trunk` - Open the vehicle's trunk if the vehicle is unlocked, allowing outside access to items.
- `/vehitems` (`/vinv`, `/vitems`) - List all the current items in the vehicle.
- `/vtitem <item id> (<amount>)` - Take an item from the vehicle (vehicle take).
- `/vpitem <item id> (<amount>)` - Place an item in the vehicle (vehicle put).
- `/vtitems` - Take all items from the vehicle, limited to what you can carry on you.
- `/vpitems` - Place all your items in a vehicle, limited to what the vehicle can store.
