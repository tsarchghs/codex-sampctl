# Residential garages

Feature showcase content for GTA World Forums: GTA V Heavy Roleplay Server.

## Commands

- **Press Y** – Enter/exit an unlocked garage while on foot.
- **Press K** – Enter/exit an unlocked garage while on foot or in a vehicle.
- Remember where your entrance is to get back out.
- `/plock` – Lock or unlock a garage (tenants only).
- **Press I** – Open the general menu. Page 1 for inventory and `/pmenu`, page 2 for the construction menu to add props and furniture.
- `/pentrance` – Change the inside entrance of the garage to where you stand. Needs to be in an open space for vehicles.
- `/pinv` – Check the inventory of a garage (tenants only).
- `/ptitem` or `/ptitems` – Remove an item from the garage. Adding `S` removes as many as possible (tenants only).
- `/ppitem` or `/ppitems` – Place an item in the garage. Adding `S` places as many as possible (tenants only).
- `/outfit` – Open the menu to switch between saved outfits (tenants only).
- `/pmenu` – Information menu, alternate method to `/plock` and inventory commands. Allow construction rights to tenants.
- `/pinfo` – Show garage information (owner only).
- `/setrentable` – Make the garage available for rent. Enter without a price to stop allowing new tenants.
- `/rent` – Rent a garage.
- `/stoprent` – Stop renting. Use at the outside door for best results.
- `/tenants` – Check tenants in the property (owner only).
- `/kicktenant` – Remove a specific tenant from the garage (owner only).
- `/evictall` – Remove all tenants from the garage (owner only).
- `/pdeposit` – Place money in the garage money box (owner only).
- `/pwithdrawl` – Take money from the garage money box (owner only).
- `/sellproperty` – Sell your garage back to the market when selling the house (half value if applicable) (owner only).
- `/playersellproperty` – Sell your garage to a specific player when selling the house (owner only).

## Requesting a garage

If a property has no garage, you can request one via Property Management if your house has a suitable exterior garage entrance.
